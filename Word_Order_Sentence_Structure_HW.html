Gap Fill — Complete the Sentences
Choose a word from the bank and type it in the correct gap.
10 points
📖 Grammar Reminder / Dilbilgisi Hatırlatması
Frequency adverbs (always, usually, never) go before the main verb.
Question words (what, where, when) go first in the sentence.
Do is used with I / you / we / they.
Does is used with he / she / it.
Sıklık zarfları (always/her zaman, usually/genellikle, never/asla) ana fiilden önce gelir.
Soru kelimeleri (what/ne, where/nerede, when/ne zaman) cümlede en başa gelir.
Do: I / you / we / they ile kullanılır.
Does: he / she / it ile kullanılır.
✅ She always checks the report.
✅ Where do you work?
✅ Does he speak English?
✅ Raporu her zaman kontrol eder.
✅ Nerede çalışıyorsunuz?
✅ İngilizce konuşuyor mu?
⚠️ Don't / Doesn't = negative. Do / Does = question. Never use both at the same time! / Don't/Doesn't = olumsuz. Do/Does = soru. İkisini aynı anda kullanmayın!

Error Correction — Fix the Sentence
Each sentence has one mistake. Write the corrected sentence below.
6 points
📖 Grammar Reminder / Dilbilgisi Hatırlatması
There is exactly one mistake in each sentence. It will be one of these:
1. Missing -s on he/she/it verb
2. Wrong negative form (should be don't/doesn't)
3. Wrong question word order
4. Using -ing instead of Present Simple
5. Wrong subject-verb order
Her cümlede tam olarak bir hata var. Bunlardan biri olacak:
1. He/She/It fiiline -s eksik
2. Yanlış olumsuz biçim (don't/doesn't olmalı)
3. Soruda yanlış kelime sırası
4. Geniş Zaman yerine -ing kullanımı
5. Yanlış özne-fiil sırası
🔍 Read each sentence slowly. Ask: "Does the verb agree with the subject? Is the word order correct?" / Her cümleyi yavaşça okuyun. Sorun: "Fiil özneyle uyuşuyor mu? Kelime sırası doğru mu?"
❌ "She work in the maintenance department."
Hint: he/she/it → verb + s
❌ "Does they check the engines every week?"
Hint: "they" uses do, not does.
❌ "I am usually drive to work."
Hint: For habits, we use Present Simple — not am/is/are + verb-ing.
❌ "He go not to the office on Fridays."
Hint: To make a negative with he/she/it → doesn't + base verb.
❌ "What time you start work?"
Hint: We need a helper verb (do/does) in questions.
❌ "The captain every morning to the hangar goes."
Hint: S + V + Object + Place — the verb comes right after the subject.

📖 Grammar Reminder / Dilbilgisi Hatırlatması
Remember the correct English word order:
Positive: Subject + Verb + Object + Place + Time
Negative: Subject + don't/doesn't + Verb + Object
Question: Wh-word + do/does + Subject + Verb?
Doğru İngilizce kelime sırasını hatırlayın:
Olumlu: Özne + Fiil + Nesne + Yer + Zaman
Olumsuz: Özne + don't/doesn't + Fiil + Nesne
Soru: Soru kelimesi + do/does + Özne + Fiil?
✅ My wife cooks dinner at home every evening.
✅ What does the engineer do in the morning?
✅ Eşim her akşam evde akşam yemeği pişirir.
✅ Mühendis sabahları ne yapar?
