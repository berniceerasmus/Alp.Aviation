Sentence Building — Your Own Words
Use the prompts to write a complete sentence. Then say it aloud!
⏱ 12 min
📖 Grammar Reminder / Dilbilgisi Hatırlatması
Use this formula to build your sentence:
Positive: I/He/She + verb(+s) + object + place + time
Negative: I + don't / He + doesn't + verb + object
Question: What/Where + do/does + subject + verb?
Cümle kurmak için bu formülü kullanın:
Olumlu: Ben/O + fiil(+s) + nesne + yer + zaman
Olumsuz: Ben + don't / O + doesn't + fiil + nesne
Soru: What/Where + do/does + özne + fiil?
💡 Your sentence doesn't need to be long — just correct! A short, correct sentence is better than a long, wrong one. / Cümlenizin uzun olması gerekmez — sadece doğru olsun! Kısa ve doğru, uzun ve yanlıştan iyidir.
Teacher note: After writing, each person reads their sentence aloud. The group can ask one question about it. Mistakes are welcome — that's how we learn!
1. Write a sentence about what you do every morning before work.
Use: Subject + Verb + Object + Time
💡 Example: "I drink one coffee and check my phone every morning."
2. Write a sentence about something you don't do at work.
Use: Subject + don't/doesn't + Verb + Object
💡 Example: "I don't eat lunch in the office — I go outside."
3. Write a question to ask your partner about their weekend routine.
Use: What / Where / When + do/does + Subject + Verb?
💡 Example: "What do you usually do on Sunday mornings?"
4. Write a sentence about a colleague or someone on your team — what they do at work.
Use: He/She + Verb(+s) + Object + Place
💡 Example: "He checks the engines in the maintenance hangar every week."

Yes/No or Wh- Question?
Look at each situation. What kind of question do you need? Click the right type.
⏱ 8 min
📖 Grammar Reminder / Dilbilgisi Hatırlatması
Yes/No Question: The answer is YES or NO.
→ Use Do / Does at the start.
Does she work here? → Yes, she does.

Wh- Question: The answer gives information.
→ Use What / Where / When / How at the start.
Where does she work? → In the hangar.
Evet/Hayır Sorusu: Cevap EVET ya da HAYIR'dır.
→ Başta Do / Does kullanılır.
Burada mı çalışıyor? → Evet, çalışıyor.

Soru Kelimeli Soru: Cevap bilgi verir.
→ Başta What/Nerede/Ne zaman/Nasıl kullanılır.
Nerede çalışıyor? → Hangarda.
⚠️ Ask yourself: "Can I answer this with just YES or NO?" If yes → Yes/No question. If you need more info → Wh- question. / Kendinize sorun: "Buna sadece EVET ya da HAYIR ile cevap verebilir miyim?" Evet → Evet/Hayır sorusu. Daha fazla bilgi gerekiyorsa → Soru kelimeli soru.
Teacher note: After each answer, ask the group to actually SAY the question. This is the best speaking practice in the activity!
You want to know the time the shift starts.
You want to know if your colleague works today.
You want to know where the manager eats lunch.
You want to know if the pilot speaks English.
You want to know how often they service the aircraft.
You want to know if the office is open on Saturdays.
